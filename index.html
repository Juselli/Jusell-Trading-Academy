Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our trading programs

Do I need prior trading experience to join?

Experience level doesn't matter - I've worked with complete beginners and experienced traders. What matters is your motivation, commitment to learning, and willingness to challenge yourself. I'm selective about who I accept based on mindset, not background.

How much money do I need to start trading?

We recommend starting with at least $1,000-$5,000 for live trading, but you can practice with paper trading (virtual money) while learning. The key is to start with money you can afford to lose while you're learning and growing your skills.

Do you guarantee profits?

We cannot guarantee profits as trading involves risk. However, we do guarantee that you'll receive comprehensive education and support. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the program quality and content.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time availability isn't the deciding factor - your dedication is. Whether you have 30 minutes or 3 hours daily, what matters is that you're genuinely committed to growth and willing to put in consistent effort during whatever time you have available.

What markets do you teach?

I mainly trade futures myself, but the market doesn't matter. The principles I teach - psychology, risk management, and developing your own style - work across all markets: forex, stocks, crypto, commodities, and futures. You'll learn universal trading concepts that apply anywhere there's price movement.

I'm selective about who I work with. This consultation helps determine if you have the right mindset and motivation for serious trading development - regardless of your current experience level.
