Execution Drills

Short, focused exercises to improve timing and rule‑following without risking much capital.

Replay Drill (15–20 min)

  • Pick one instrument and one day. Replay at 1–2x speed.
  • Take 10 simulated entries of the same setup only.
  • Screenshot best/worst; note trigger, stop, and timing.

Target: learn where timing is too early/late.

Timer Drill (Momentum Patience)

  • When price reaches your zone, start a 3‑minute timer.
  • Enter only if your trigger appears before time runs out.
  • If not, cancel. No chasing.

Target: reduce impulse entries.

Checklist Drill (3 Conditions)

  • Write 3 must‑have conditions for your setup.
  • Only click when all 3 are true; verbalize them before entry.
  • Track results for 20 trades.

Target: strengthen rule‑following.

Weekly Plan

  • Mon–Tue: Replay drill (10 entries per day)
  • Wed: Timer drill live (tiny size)
  • Thu: Checklist drill (20 trades over time)
  • Fri: Review screenshots; update one micro‑rule
